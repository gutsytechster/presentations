What is time tracking?

  • Keeping the track of time spent on various tasks or projects.
    •
  • Used to track the working hours of employees.
    •
  • Used to generate weekly/monthly invoices based on working hours over a project.
    •

What are timesheets?

  • These are sheets to record the number of hours worked across projects in a day.
    •
  • It is an implementation of time tracking.
    •
  • Used to analyze how much hours an employee worked over a project in a week/month.
    •

What is forecast?

  • It is a service to schedule upcoming projects.
    •
  • It analyse timesheets to show employees' time availablity.
    •

What is standup?

  1. An approach of Scrum methodology in Agile development
    2.
  2. In this, team members give updates of their work to the team leader
    3.
