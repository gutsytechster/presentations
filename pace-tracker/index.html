What is time tracking?

  • The measurement and documentation of hours worked
  • Recording of time, spent on tasks or project
  • Used widely by organizations to record the working hours of hourly employees

What are timesheets?

It is the record of amount of time a person spent on each task

It is simply used for time tracking

What is forecasting?

Evaluation of future tasks based on current timesheets is what we call as forecasting

What is standup?

  1. Stand-up is a meeting in which attendees typically participate while standing
  2. An approach of Scrum methodology in Agile development
  3. A solution to lead a team with grace and efficiency
